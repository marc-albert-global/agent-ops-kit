When analyzing recurring revenue:

1. Decompose Net New MRR into its four components and always report them
   separately: **New** (net-new logos), **Expansion** (upsell/cross-sell on
   existing accounts), **Contraction** (downgrades), and **Churn** (lost
   accounts). Net New MRR = New + Expansion − Contraction − Churn.
2. Lead with Net New MRR, then show the decomposition — a flat headline number
   hides whether growth is healthy (new + expansion) or propped up by avoiding
   churn.
3. Quote ARR as MRR × 12 only for run-rate framing; never mix booked ARR and
   run-rate ARR in the same table without labeling which is which.
4. State the period and comparison base for every figure.
